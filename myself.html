The Salt in the Soul

Born and raised amidst the volcanic landscapes and the infinite Atlantic horizon of Lanzarote, my relationship with the ocean is not just a preference, but a fundamental part of my identity. From a young age, the rhythmic motion of the waves and the raw, untamed beauty of the Canary Islands have shaped my aesthetic sensibilities and my understanding of light.

To me, photography and filmmaking are extensions of this lifelong observation. I seek to capture the silent strength of the coast and the fleeting moments where human emotion meets the vastness of the natural world. My work is a tribute to minimalism, texture, and the honesty of a single frame. I don't just take pictures; I look for the narrative hidden within the silence of the sea.

Based in my homeland but driven by a global curiosity, I specialize in creating visual content that resonates on a deeper level. Whether it is through high-end fashion editorials, surf culture documentation, or commercial storytelling, I bring the intensity and clarity of my island's heritage to every project I undertake.
